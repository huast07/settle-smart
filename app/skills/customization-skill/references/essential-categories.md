# Essential Category Heuristics

Use this reference to refine category selection when demographics make the default essentials too broad.

## Household Composition

- Single adult: prioritize groceries, transit, banking, pharmacy, mobile/internet setup, credit-building, and rent/utility discounts.
- Family with children: add family transit fares, child recreation discounts, school supplies, kids clothing, grocery family offers, and pharmacy essentials.
- Large household of four or more: add bulk household purchasing, warehouse clubs, recurring delivery discounts, multi-line telecom discounts, and multi-person transit options.
- Older adult or senior: add senior transit, pharmacy, grocery, banking, community center, and utility discount searches.
- Student or youth: add student transit, campus banking, youth recreation, mobile plans, and education supplies.

## Priority Guidance

- `high`: immediate recurring cost or urgent access need in the first 30-60 days.
- `medium`: valuable after core setup, or depends on eligibility, fees, or user preference.
- `low`: useful but nonessential, highly speculative, or mostly lifestyle-oriented.

## Search Focus Examples

- Food and groceries: supermarket loyalty, coupon app, weekly flyer rewards, pharmacy grocery points.
- Transit and mobility: transit pass discount, newcomer fare, low-income fare, family pass, bike share membership.
- Banking: newcomer account bonus, no-fee account, debit rewards, remittance offer.
- Credit-building: secured credit card, newcomer credit card, no annual fee rewards card.
- Healthcare/pharmacy: commercial pharmacy rewards, prescription savings card, government healthcare benefits, public prescription drug benefit.
- Housing/utilities: rent rewards, utility discount, tenant benefit, internet affordability program.
- Cellular/telecommunications: mobile carrier rewards, prepaid phone plan discount, multi-line plan discount, internet affordability program, bundle rewards.
